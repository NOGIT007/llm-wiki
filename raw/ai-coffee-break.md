# AI Coffee Break

Thirty minutes a month. That's all it takes to stay AI-fluent. AI Coffee Break is a monthly live session where you hear what's actually happening in AI right now — new tools, real use cases from inside Visma, and practical takeaways you can apply the same day.

No prep. No homework. No prerequisites. Just grab a coffee and show up.

## What You Get

- **15-minute presentation** on a current AI topic — tools, trends, or real-world examples from Visma teams
- **15-minute open Q&A** — ask anything about AI, no question too basic or too advanced
- **Recording shared afterwards** — miss the live session, watch it later

Every session is designed to give you one clear thing you can try or think about differently. You walk away smarter in half an hour.

## How It Works

Sessions run **every second Wednesday of the month** at **14:00 FI / 13:00 CET**, from March through December (July off for summer). That's **9 sessions in 2026**.

Each one follows the same simple format:

1. A short, focused talk on what's new and relevant in AI
2. Open floor for questions — from "What tool should I try?" to "How does this affect my team?"
3. Recording and key takeaways shared to everyone afterwards

## Who It's For

Everyone at Visma. Whether you're a developer experimenting with AI-assisted coding, a team lead evaluating new tools, or someone who's just curious — there's something here for you.

AI Coffee Break is the lowest-barrier entry point to AI learning at Visma. If you attend nothing else, attend this.

## Why It Matters

AI moves fast. What was cutting-edge six months ago is now table stakes. Without a regular touchpoint, it's easy to fall behind — and harder to catch up.

AI Coffee Break keeps you in the loop with minimal time investment. It's not a course. It's not a commitment. It's a monthly check-in that compounds over time.
