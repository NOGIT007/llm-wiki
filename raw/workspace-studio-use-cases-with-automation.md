> ## **Google Workspace Studio:What Can You Actually Automate?**
>
> A practical playbook for identifying which workflows are ready for AI automation today — and which ones still need a human in the loop.

Not every workflow is ready for AI automation — but many are. The gap between what AI agents promise and what they actually deliver is real, and closing it starts with knowing **which tasks to hand off** and **how much oversight each one needs**.

This use case playbook breaks down real-world workflows you can automate with **Google Workspace Studio** right now, organized into three reliability tiers. You will learn which tasks you can set and forget, which ones need a quick review before sending, and which ones should always have a human making the final call. Interactive quizzes throughout help you pressure-test your judgment before you build.
