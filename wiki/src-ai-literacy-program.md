## Summary

The AI Literacy Program is Visma's overarching learning framework for building organization-wide AI fluency. It moves beyond awareness to role-specific competence, with security, compliance, and ethics integrated throughout rather than bolted on as afterthoughts.

## Structure

### Foundation Course
A self-paced course covering what every employee needs: AI capabilities and limitations, security awareness, responsible use principles. Strongly recommended for all employees, with tracked completion and certification.

### Role-Specific Deep Dives
Self-paced modules developed with Visma Security and Legal teams, each ending with certification. Rollout priority:
1. **Leaders** -- AI governance, risk management, team enablement
2. **Sales, HR, Finance, Customer Success, Project Management** -- Role-tailored tools, data handling, audit implications
3. **Marketing** -- Content compliance, brand safety, GDPR

### Weekly Blogs
At least one post per week covering [[AI Security]] news, policy updates, practical tips, and tool-specific guidance. Published on the Emerging Technology Unit website with contributions from Security and Legal teams.

### Ethics Integration
Ethics is woven into every learning path: a dedicated foundation module, role-specific ethical scenarios in every deep dive, and practical frameworks for bias awareness, transparency, disclosure, and human accountability.

## EU AI Act Alignment

Article 4 of the EU AI Act introduces expectations around AI knowledge for providers and deployers in the AI value chain. A structured program with tracked completions and verifiable certifications directly addresses these regulatory expectations.
