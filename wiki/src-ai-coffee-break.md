## Summary

AI Coffee Break is a monthly 30-minute live webinar designed as the lowest-barrier entry point to AI learning at Visma. It keeps all employees informed about AI developments with minimal time investment, following a consistent format of presentation plus open Q&A.

## Format

Each session runs every second Wednesday of the month (March through December, July off) and follows a fixed structure:

- **15-minute presentation** on a current AI topic -- tools, trends, or real use cases from Visma teams
- **15-minute open Q&A** -- any AI question welcome, from basic to advanced
- **Recording shared afterwards** for asynchronous viewing

The program delivers 9 sessions across 2026. Every session aims to give attendees one clear, actionable takeaway.

## Audience and Purpose

Designed for everyone at Visma regardless of role or technical level. The explicit goal is to prevent the knowledge gap that forms when AI moves faster than individual learning. The program frames AI fluency as something that compounds over time through regular, low-effort touchpoints.

## Key Design Principles

- No prep, homework, or prerequisites required
- Practical focus: real use cases and tools, not theory
- Monthly cadence creates sustained awareness without course commitment
