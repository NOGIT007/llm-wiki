## Overview

OpenAI is an AI company and the creator of the GPT model family and ChatGPT. In the corpus it appears primarily as a reference point for benchmarking, economic analysis, and agent reliability comparisons alongside [[Anthropic]].

## Role in the Corpus

1. **GDPval Benchmark** -- OpenAI's models feature in the GDPval displacement analysis, which estimates the economic value of AI capabilities relative to human labor across task categories.

2. **Agent Monitoring Data** -- Alongside [[Anthropic]], OpenAI's coding agents provide monitoring data used in analyses of AI agent misalignment, revealing patterns of task drift, silent failures, and capability overestimation.

3. **Commoditization Thesis** -- OpenAI is central to the "intelligence as a commodity" argument -- the idea that raw model capability is rapidly becoming undifferentiated, shifting competitive advantage toward tooling, integration, and workflow design.
