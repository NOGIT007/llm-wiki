# Mastering Nano Banana 2

[[Nano Banana 2]] (technically Gemini 3.1 Flash Image) launched February 26, 2026 as [[Google]]'s latest AI image model inside the [[Gemini]] app. It represents a leap from "AI-generated" to professional-grade output at zero cost within the app.

## Core Superpowers

Unlike older models that approximate visuals, Nano Banana 2 is a **reasoning-first** image model that plans scenes before rendering. Its six key capabilities are: (1) visual reasoning with realistic physics, (2) **search grounding** that pulls live data from Google Search for accurate infographics, (3) precision typography that finally spells correctly, (4) character consistency across up to five characters, (5) native 4K output across 14 aspect ratios, and (6) pro-quality output in under two seconds.

## The Director's Method

The recommended prompting framework treats the model like a Creative Director conversation, not a programming interface. The structure follows: Subject + Composition + Action + Location + Style + Format. For example, specifying lens type, aperture, lighting direction, and resolution yields professional product shots. This approach aligns with broader [[Prompt Engineering]] principles — specificity drives quality.

## Business Applications

Five practical use cases include: data infographics with search-grounded statistics, DIY product photography with background removal, sales dashboard visualizations from raw spreadsheets, visual PRD storyboarding, and event poster creation with perfect typography.

## Learning Hack: YouTube-to-Sketchnote

A two-step workflow summarizes video content into visual sketchnotes: first extract 5-7 actionable steps from a YouTube URL, then generate a whiteboard-style graphic recording. This turns hour-long videos into one-page visual summaries in about 60 seconds — a powerful example of [[AI Agents]] augmenting learning workflows.
