## Overview

Visma is a Nordic enterprise software organization that appears in the corpus through its internal AI training and adoption programs. Rather than building AI models, Visma focuses on upskilling its workforce to use AI tools effectively.

## Programs in the Corpus

- **AI Coffee Break** -- Short-format, recurring sessions designed to lower the barrier to AI adoption. These informal touchpoints introduce employees to practical AI use cases without requiring deep technical background.
- **AI Literacy Program** -- A structured curriculum for building organization-wide AI fluency. Covers foundational concepts, tool proficiency, and judgment development so employees can evaluate when and how to apply AI to their work.

## Role in the Corpus

Visma represents the enterprise adoption perspective -- how large organizations move from individual experimentation to systematic AI capability building. Its programs illustrate the [[Work Structure]] principle that tooling alone is insufficient; organizations need deliberate training infrastructure to capture value from AI.
